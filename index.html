What is ANWJr?

ANWJr is a head to head obstacle course racing show. It is a spin off of the popular TV show American Ninja Warrior. The kids race on a side by side course filled with obstacles seen on the adult show. There are three age groups 9-10, 11-12 and 13-14. In each age group the top four kids get a cash prize. First place wins 15,000 dollars.

Where and how should I train for ANW?

There are many gyms across the country and ways to train at home mudrunguide.com is a great website to search for gyms near you. Buying a pull up bar could be a good option because building up grip and arm strength is very important. Buying ninja holds from somewhere like atomicclimbingholds.com or monstro.ninja could be options but you can also try to make some on your own. Working on overall fitness is very helpful as well as core and endurance. Simple workouts like lunge jumps, push ups and pull ups (wide grip or narrow) are great for at home workouts. Rock climbing is a great sport to get into if there isn't a ninja gym near you, or if you want to mix it up.



Numerous people in the ninja and rock climbing community also do acro yoga. It trains strength, balance and flexibility. To start out with your training with ninja look for a gym near you and take a class. There are many videos on how to have the right form or fun workouts to do at home or at the gym.
